Why #HackFrankfurt?

The right mixture of people (incl. awesome mentors) and sensor hardware will be present (ideally bring yours) to make this hackathon a nice opportunity to help work on applications for citizens that inspect the air quality of our city.

Basically Smart City and IoT but with the citizens and privacy in mind.

We dislike surveillance as well and favor high privacy standards. So if people want to make it useful only for them, that's OK. Help us publish air quality data, protect citizen privacy, tinker on sensor kits, visualize real-time data and enable analyses for the greater good. Everybody can make a difference.

Whether it's a merely somebody caring for air quality or someone who'll jump right into these topics.
