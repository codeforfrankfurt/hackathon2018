The Open Data Day 2018

Open Public Data is the entrance not only to deep knowledge and understanding but also to collaborative actions that can have huge social impact on our life as citizens.

Developers, Designers, Librarians, Statisticians, Citizen – everybody can make a difference and is heartily welcome to the opendataday.org

Open Knowledge is a worldwide non-profit network of people passionate about openness, using advocacy, technology and training to unlock information and enable people to work with it to create and share knowledge. okfn.de

Your local Hackathon Host

Why #HackFrankfurt?

A lot of questions make this hackathon a perfect opportunity to prove that by writing applications, liberating data, creating visualizations and publising analyses for the greater good everybody can make a difference.

Because citizenship can mean much more than just to cast one’s vote.

What’s your open data idea?

If you like to start by getting some very first inspiration have a look a codefor.de. If you rather take off from a data point of view visit offenedaten.frankfurt.de. And if you are more into some real hands on stuff check out the three cases we researched and identified as great examples which you probably will find as interesting as we do.
