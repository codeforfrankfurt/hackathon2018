Why #HackFrankfurt?

The right mixture of people and sensor hardware will be present (ideally bring yours) to make this hackathon a nice opportunity to help work on applications for citizens that inspect the air quality of our city.

Basically Smart City but with the citizens and privacy in mind.

We dislike cloud surveillance as well and if people want to make it useful only for them, that's OK. Help publish air quality data, protect citizen data, tinker on sensor kits, visualize real-time data, enable analyses for the greater good. Everybody can make a difference.

Whether it's a merely somebody caring for air quality or someone who'll jump right into these topics.

Because citizenship can mean much more than just to cast one’s vote.
